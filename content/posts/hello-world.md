This is the beginning of osousa.com — my personal space to write about cybersecurity,
networking, and whatever I happen to be learning or working on.

I'm a Cybersecurity Analyst based in Mozambique, currently working at Hidroeléctrica
de Cahora Bassa. After 20 years in IT across systems analysis, networking, and service
desk management, I've recently moved fully into security — and there's always something
new to learn, document, and share.

## What to expect here

- Writeups on security topics, tools, and techniques
- Notes from training and certifications
- Observations from working in security within critical infrastructure
- The occasional post on networking, Linux, or systems

No ads, no tracking, no fluff. Just notes from the field.
